Job Interview


It's time to shine!

Be prepared

Make sure you know where you're going and how to get there
If you have been asked to bring anything with you, make sure you have it

Pay some attention to the way you look

Wash, Shave, Do your hair

Make sure you are looking your best

Dress to Impress

Wear a Shirt, Tie, Trousers and Shoes

An interviewer will not be impressed if you turn up wearing a tracksuit and trainers

Ask for help if you need it

The Job Centre may be able to help you with costs towards suitable interview clothes and travel expenses

Do some research on your potential employer

This will help you in answering some of the questions they may ask

Knowing about their business also helps you to appear keen

Prepare some questions for you to ask them too

An interview is not just for your potential employer to find out about you
It is also your chance to find out a little about them

Focus

Make eye contact

Smile

Try to appear friendly and relaxed

 

End on a positive note

This could be as simple as saying

"I look forward to hearing from you"

Shake your interviewers hand before leaving

How well have you done?
